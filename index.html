About Me

Organic growth that shows up where your buyers actually search

I'm an SEO Specialist focused on organic growth, SERP visibility, AI search discovery, and conversion-driven content. I approach SEO as a system: starting with audits and SERP analysis, mapping keywords to the right pages, optimising technical foundations, and iterating using Search Console data until content ranks, gets discovered in AI search, and supports conversions.

What sets my work apart is that I optimise for both traditional Google results and AI-generated answers. As ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews become primary discovery surfaces, I structure content so brands show up there too, not just in the blue links.

I've shipped a 17-piece content cluster that drove a high-intent keyword from position 25 to consistent Top 3–5 in 8 weeks. I've resolved 40+ technical SEO issues contributing to a 25% improvement in site performance. And I've optimised 150+ commercial pages across SaaS, e-commerce, and travel brands.
