\#\#\# Case Study 3: 340% Click Growth & $557 Organic Revenue for E-commerce Brand  
\- Client: Knotwtr  
\- Industry: E-commerce  
\- Situation: Despite having an active store, Knotwtr had no optimized  
  product or category pages and very little content. Search engines  
  couldn't properly understand or index their offerings, which meant  
  qualified buyers were finding competitors instead.  
\- What I did: Rebuilt the entire SEO strategy from scratch. Optimized  
  all product and category pages for search intent and conversions.  
  Implemented structured data (schema markup) so Google and LLMs could  
  better understand and surface their products. Created a pillar and  
  cluster content strategy to drive qualified organic traffic directly  
  to product and category pages.  
\- Results:  
  \- Total clicks grew from 777 to 3.42K (+340% year over year)  
  \- Impressions grew from 57.1K to 210K (+267%)  
  \- Average position improved from 44 to 37.9  
  \- Organic search generated $557.58 in revenue and 7 conversions  
    in Q4 2025 alone  
\- Timeline: \~12 months (ongoing contract)  
\- Services: Technical SEO, On-Page SEO, Content Strategy, Schema Markup  
\- Image: case-study-knotwtr
